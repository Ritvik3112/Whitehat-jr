history

  • a slow start to a start in develpoing

    Our wonderful school started with a slow start in 1957. When our beloved dean and director Dr.Mrs.YGP mam decided to unearth the knowledge of children across India and the world, she started with only 13 students in a hut. Imagine! From then, the number slowly escalated to 500 and she didn't have any more room for her students. So one of her best friend Mrs.Seshdri generously offered a plot of land back in 1958 to build the school there and that's why the school is named after her Padma Seshadri Bala Bhavan (PSBB).

  • branch after branch

    After getting the geneous offer from Mrs. Seshadri, Mrs. YGP started gathering a couple of workers to construct the building in Nungambakkam. It was a tedious project which started in 1958 and ended one year later in 1959. In 1971, there was a need to construct another branch and was successfull completed in TP Road, T Nagar, Chennai. In 1976 there came a branch in KK Nagar! Wow! Look at the motivation by our YGP mam! 13 students in a hut to a massive group of schools with four branches each having over 40000 students!

campus life

YGP auditorium

a place to explore people's talents

This auditorium is a wonderful place to show children's talent in quizzing and theatre. It is one of the pillars of our school campus and it isnamed after our beloved dean and director Dr. Mrs YGP mam. This is a beautiful and well-maintained air-conditioned hall which has two-storeys where we can even project movies!

the school swimming pool

a place to swim in peace

The school swimming pool is found just opposite to the second block of PSBB KK nagar school. It has two fitting rooms on both sides of the pool where you can shower and change. Here we can master the arts of swimming which is the most important life skill of life. We have teachers who can take care of us and also teach us tricks on this life skill.

the A-Z lending library

a heaven of books

When you enter this library, you will think that you have descended into a heaven of books. You will see books on all sides, covering the topics of fiction, fantasy, english, science, social, you name it neatly arranged in order. I assure you, anybody who enters this library will not leave empty-handed.
